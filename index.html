About Me

A Passion for Purposeful Celebrations

I am a wedding enthusiast at heart — someone who genuinely loves every dimension of what makes a celebration memorable. From the intricate details of décor and table settings to the warmth of a perfectly managed guest experience, I believe celebrations are a deeply human art form.

With a strong eye for décor aesthetics, food & beverage presentation, clothing sensibilities, and gifting coordination, I bring a holistic understanding of the Indian wedding ecosystem. My technical background from a prior corporate career has given me exceptional organisational discipline, attention to timelines, and a structured approach to even the most emotionally complex events.

I made a conscious decision to leave a well-paying corporate career to pursue something that fills me with genuine meaning — creating beautiful moments for people on the most important days of their lives. I planned my own wedding, coordinated celebrations for family and friends, and every experience only deepened my commitment to this profession.

"Great celebrations are never just organised. They are thoughtfully felt."

My Philosophy

An Approach Rooted in Heart & Precision

Every celebration deserves more than efficient management — it deserves genuine attention, emotional intelligence, and the kind of care that turns moments into lifelong memories. I bring an ownership mindset to everything I do; I don't manage events, I take responsibility for them.

My background in a high-performance corporate environment trained me to stay composed under pressure, to think systemically, and to anticipate problems before they arise. But it's my love for people and hospitality that transforms that structure into warmth.

I approach every client relationship as a partnership — listening deeply, communicating clearly, and delivering not just what was promised, but what was truly felt. When a couple says their day was everything they imagined, that is the only metric that matters.
